Time Travel Basics
This is not a time travel machine. 
It is the simulation of a parellel time-line where users can experience and alternative reality. 
This means users can go back in time and make different choices and play the simulation of an alternative present. 
Or, users can go to future and play the simulation of an alternative future that AI predicted which might happen to them in the future based on the anlaysis of their previous choices and lifestyle.
